# Bioinformatics Audit Prep Notes – ProvLab

## Main Focus Areas

* Strong emphasis on documentation:

  * SOPs
  * Problem troubleshooting records
  * Software maintenance and updates
  * Training documentation and competency records
  * Records showing qualification/authorization to run pipelines

---

## Audit Discussion Topics

* Detailed questions about:

  * PathogenSeq workflow and components
  * QC matrices and QC thresholds
  * Different tools used for different data types
  * How tools/databases are selected and updated

---

## Workflow Walkthrough

Be prepared to walk through a typical analysis step-by-step:

1. Receiving raw data
2. Running the pipeline
3. Reviewing logs and outputs
4. QC evaluation and thresholds
5. Downstream analysis and reporting

### Auditor Focus

* Understanding the full process and traceability
* What happens downstream after data is received
* How outputs and QC metrics are reviewed
* How failures or threshold issues are handled
