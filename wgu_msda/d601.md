[Communication is a Data Analyst’s Most Important Soft Skill](https://cusomag.com/2023/04/17/communication-is-the-most-important-soft-skill/)
- If you can write it, you can communicate it
- Strategies for building communication skills
  - The number one way I have found success in developing my own communication skills as an analyst is via writing and re-writing what I am explaining.
  - The other strategy to use when trying to develop stronger communication skills is to talk with someone or attempt to train someone in a different role from yourself.
